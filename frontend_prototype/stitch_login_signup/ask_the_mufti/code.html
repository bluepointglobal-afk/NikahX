What are the rights of a wife regarding housing in Islam?

10:42 AM
smart_toy
Mufti AI Beta

In Islamic jurisprudence, a wife has the definitive right to separate housing (Sukna). It must be safe, secure, and suitable for her social status.

Crucially, this housing must be free from the husband's family members unless she voluntarily agrees to live with them. The privacy of the wife is paramount.
